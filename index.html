Myth: "Only Trees Count"

False. While forests are vital, Grasslands are often more reliable carbon sinks because they store 90% of their carbon underground in roots and soil, making them resilient to wildfires.

The Soil & Blue Carbon

The "VeraCarbon" Standard

We align with Verra (VCS), the world's leading standard. It is not just about planting; it is about proof.

1. Additionality: Would this carbon removal have happened anyway? If yes, no credit.
2. Permanence: Will the carbon stay locked away for 100+ years?
3. Independent Audit: Third-party auditors (VVBs) must verify our AI data physically.
